# Game Vision

## Vision Statement
Create a polished Tower Defense game in Unity where players use strategic tower placement, upgrades, and resource management to stop increasingly difficult enemy waves.

## Player Fantasy
The player acts as a battlefield planner who builds a defensive network and adapts to changing enemy threats.

## Experience Goals
- Easy to understand
- Tactically satisfying
- Clear combat feedback
- Meaningful progression
- Increasing challenge without unfairness

## Design Pillars
1. Strategic placement matters
2. Enemy variety creates decision pressure
3. Upgrades are meaningful choices
4. Resources create tension and planning
5. Systems should be modular and extensible

## Scope Direction
This is a systems-first project emphasizing gameplay logic, structured design, and a playable executable build.
