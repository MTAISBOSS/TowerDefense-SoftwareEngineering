# Art Direction

## Note
The proposal page retrieved does not define art style, so this section is a recommended design direction rather than a direct requirement.

## Recommended Style
Stylized low-poly / clean readable 3D

## Reasons
- Good fit for Unity student/indie scope
- Easy to read during fast wave gameplay
- Efficient production pipeline
- Works well for modular environments and towers

## Visual Priorities
- Clear enemy silhouettes
- Distinct tower archetypes
- Visible attack ranges and projectile types
- Easy recognition of special enemies
- Strong contrast between path and build zones

## Color Language
- Friendly structures: blue/green
- Enemy units: red/orange/purple
- Frozen/slow states: cyan
- Explosive/AOE effects: yellow/orange

## Environment Themes
- Grassland
- Canyon
- Fortress approach
