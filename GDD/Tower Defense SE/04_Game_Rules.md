# Game Rules

## Basic Rules
- Enemies spawn in waves
- Enemies follow a path to a destination
- Towers can be placed on valid build nodes
- Towers automatically attack enemies in range
- Defeated enemies reward currency
- Escaped enemies reduce player health
- Player loses when health reaches zero
- Player wins when all waves are cleared

## Tower Rules
- Towers cost resources to place
- Towers may be upgraded to improve stats
- Each tower type has unique strengths
- Towers cannot be placed on blocked or invalid spaces

## Enemy Rules
- Enemy types differ in health, speed, armor, and special traits
- Some enemies may resist certain damage types
- Higher levels introduce stronger enemies and new vulnerabilities

## Gameplay Constraints
- Build choices are limited by available currency
- Tower placement is spatially constrained by the map
- Wave pressure increases over time
