# Audio Design

## Note
The proposal page retrieved does not specify audio direction, so this section is a recommended design layer.

## Audio Goals
- Reinforce clarity
- Improve feedback
- Increase tension during waves
- Reward upgrades and victories

## Sound Categories
- Tower fire sounds
- Enemy hit sounds
- Enemy death sounds
- UI click sounds
- Placement valid/invalid sounds
- Upgrade sounds
- Base damage warning sound
- Wave start sting
- Victory / defeat music cues

## Music Direction
- Calm planning music during build phase
- More intense loop during combat
- Stronger boss-wave variation
