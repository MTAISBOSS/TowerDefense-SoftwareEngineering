# Combat System

## Purpose
Resolves attacks, projectiles, damage, status effects, and enemy death.

## Responsibilities
- Target selection
- Fire timing
- Projectile movement
- Damage resolution
- Splash damage
- Slow effects
- Death and reward events

## Damage Types
- Physical
- Explosive
- Frost
- Optional magic/true damage in future scope

## Combat Goals
- Readable
- Responsive
- Extensible
- Easy to balance
