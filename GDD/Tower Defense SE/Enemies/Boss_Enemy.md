# Boss Enemy

## Role
Wave-ending major threat

## Stats
- Health: Very High
- Speed: Low to Medium
- Reward: High
- Threat Type: Endurance and spike challenge

## Special Traits
- May resist one damage type
- May summon support enemies
- May temporarily shield itself

## Purpose
Tests full defense efficiency and upgrade decisions

## Countered By
- Balanced tower compositions
- Strong upgrade investment
- Slow + burst synergy
