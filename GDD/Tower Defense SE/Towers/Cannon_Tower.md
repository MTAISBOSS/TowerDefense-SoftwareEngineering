# Cannon Tower

## Role
Area-of-effect damage tower

## Purpose
Handles grouped enemies and swarm waves

## Stats
- Cost: 150
- Damage: 30
- Range: Medium
- Fire Rate: Slow
- Damage Type: Explosive

## Strengths
- High splash damage
- Strong at choke points

## Weaknesses
- Slow attack speed
- Less effective against spread-out enemies

## Upgrade Path
1. Packed Shells: +AOE radius
2. Heavy Barrel: +damage
3. Shock Impact: armor reduction on hit
