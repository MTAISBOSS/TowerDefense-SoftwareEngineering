# Support Tower

## Role
Buff and utility tower

## Purpose
Improves nearby tower performance

## Stats
- Cost: 175
- Damage: 0
- Range: Aura
- Fire Rate: N/A
- Effect Type: Buff

## Effects
- Increase nearby tower fire rate
- Optional upgrade for range boost
- Optional upgrade for discount aura or bonus crit chance

## Strengths
- Strong in optimized setups
- High strategic value

## Weaknesses
- No direct damage
- Weak if placed poorly

## Upgrade Path
1. Tactical Aura: +buff strength
2. Expanded Command: +aura radius
3. Precision Orders: buff crit or damage
