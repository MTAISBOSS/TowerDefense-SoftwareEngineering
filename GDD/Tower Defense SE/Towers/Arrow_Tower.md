# Arrow Tower

## Role
Basic single-target physical damage tower

## Purpose
Reliable early-game tower effective against standard enemies

## Stats
- Cost: 100
- Damage: 15
- Range: Medium
- Fire Rate: Fast
- Damage Type: Physical

## Strengths
- Cheap
- Reliable
- Good against runners and basic enemies

## Weaknesses
- Low burst damage
- Weak against armored enemies

## Upgrade Path
1. Sharpened Arrows: +damage
2. Reinforced Bow: +range
3. Rapid Volley: +fire rate
