# Frost Tower

## Role
Crowd control tower

## Purpose
Slows enemies to give damage towers more time to attack

## Stats
- Cost: 125
- Damage: 8
- Range: Medium
- Fire Rate: Medium
- Damage Type: Frost

## Strengths
- Excellent control
- Strong utility in mixed waves
- Helps against fast enemies

## Weaknesses
- Low direct damage
- Needs synergy with damage towers

## Upgrade Path
1. Deep Chill: stronger slow
2. Wider Freeze: +range
3. Brittle Ice: enemies take bonus damage while slowed
